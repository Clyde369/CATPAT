Using technology can help people that have disabilities in learning in all kinds of ways. Such as providing reminders and making the workload lighter in certain areas physical disabilities can be assisted by using modified limbs and Assistive technology (AT) such as glasses for the visually impaired for the Sensory people they can use things such as hearing aids and the blind can use braille keyboards in order for people with low vision to use computers, or even to use larger key keyboards.
